DesignMe


My Role: Product Designer

Key Skills: Concept Ideation, Interaction Design, Visual Design, Prototyping

Intro

DesignMe was a project that I took part of while I was completing my Google UX Design certification. The idea was to design an app through which users can catalogue and purchase furniture.

The User

DesignMeâ€™s anticipated users are older, more financially stable consumers that are mildly technologically fluent. These users spend a weekly average of 17-20 hours on their phone. They use their phone for predominantly functional purposes, as opposed to leisure. These users want access to the latest furniture and corresponding prices. They are willing to invest time into downloading an app and learning how to use it.



Problem Statement

Jill is a project manager that works from her Brooklyn brownstone apartment. Since she spends most of her time at home, she wants to use some time and money to re-decorate her home office. She frequently sees ads for furniture while she is scrolling through her phone, but she is easily overwhelmed by the thousands and thousands of options. She wants an app where she can look through a more curated selection of furniture.

NEXT STEPS

Add an AR feature through which users can more accurately see what certain pieces will look like in their homes.
